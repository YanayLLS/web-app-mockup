Template Thumbnail Improvements

Current thumbnails are abstract colored rectangles. Here are three directions for improvement.

Current Thumbnail

Small abstract shapes, no text, hard to tell templates apart

→
Goal

Thumbnails that let users visually distinguish templates at a glance — showing flow shape, branching, and step count

A: Labeled Mini-Flowchart

Show actual step titles in a miniaturized flowchart. Branching shown with colored endpoints. Most informative but might get cramped with many steps.

B: Schematic with Icons

Abstract shapes (no text) but with visual cues: diamonds for decisions, icons for input types (barcode, photo), dashed connectors. Clean and scales well.

C: Polished Bar Layout

Keep the abstract style but upgrade visual quality: card-like step bars with color accents, arrow connectors, colored branch endpoints. Clean and modern.
