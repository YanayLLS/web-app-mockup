Step 3 of 10

Inspect the main feed roller assembly and check alignment

In this step, you'll inspect the main feed roller assembly located on the left side of the printing unit. Begin by opening the access panel using the release lever marked in yellow.

Once the panel is open, visually check the roller surface for any signs of wear, scoring, or ink buildup. Use the provided cleaning cloth to gently remove any residue from the roller surface.

Next, check the roller alignment by rotating it slowly by hand. The roller should spin freely without any wobble or resistance. If you notice any irregularity, note the position and proceed to the adjustment step.

Finally, verify that the tension springs on both ends of the roller are properly seated and have not lost their elasticity. Compare the spring tension with the reference gauge provided in your toolkit.
